instructions for installing the
middlepot color pack on your website
( works better on static websites,
might look messy on specific platforms )

paste this on your html,
right before closing the head tag,
just above </head> :


then you will be able to apply
the custom colors to your texts
just like regular ones, with
css style ( example : color: shy; )
or html ( example : color="shy" )
bored from using regular
plain colors in your texts ?

from my magic palette ..

delicate motifs !

it looks lovely with
girly motifs !

blue plushy texture,

it looks lovely with
cute motifs !
